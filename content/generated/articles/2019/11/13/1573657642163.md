##### 1.本地登陆 赋权
```
GRANT ALL PRIVILEGES ON *.* TO 'root'@'%' IDENTIFIED BY '密码' WITH GRANT OPTION;
 FLUSH PRIVILEGES;
```
##### 2. 修改本地绑定端口
```
/etc/mysql/**mysql.cnf
查找bind 127.0.0.1 注释掉即可
```
